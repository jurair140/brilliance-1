Description

The Plus Two in Six Months program by Brilliance Academy, conducted under the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS), is designed for students who have passed SSLC and are at least 17 years old. This flexible correspondence course allows students to complete their Senior Secondary education in just six months, preparing them for medical, engineering, IT, and other professional courses, as well as state and central government jobs.

Recognized by all major Indian universities, this program is ideal for students who discontinued Plus Two, Pre-Degree, or PUC for various reasons. Exams are held twice a year in April and October, with exam centers across Kerala, India, and Gulf affiliates in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Kuwait.

What You'll Learn in This Course?

Students will gain comprehensive knowledge in their chosen subjects, preparing them for higher education and career opportunities. The course offers flexibility with options in Science, Commerce, and Humanities streams, supported by contact classes and study materials.

  • Core Subjects (Math, Physics, Chemistry, etc.)
  • Practical Skills for Science Stream
  • Exam Preparation for NIOS
  • Career-Oriented Subject Selection
  • Eligibility for Professional Courses
  • Flexible Correspondence Learning
  • Contact Classes for Support
  • Study Materials via Post
  • Practical Lab Sessions
  • Career Guidance Support

How to Benefit from This Course

Completing the Plus Two in Six Months equips students with a Senior Secondary certificate, opening doors to medical, engineering, IT, and other professional courses. It also qualifies candidates for PSC and SSC examinations, ensuring eligibility for both state and central government jobs.
