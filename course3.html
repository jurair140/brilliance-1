Description

The BA/B.Com program by Brilliance Academy, offered through Calicut University, is designed for candidates aged 18 and above, with no requirement for formal educational qualifications like SSLC or Plus Two. This 3-year undergraduate degree program allows students to pursue higher education through a flexible learning structure, including regular classes, holiday classes for working professionals, and postal coaching.

Students must pass an entrance exam conducted by Calicut University in August to enroll. The degree is equivalent to that awarded to regular Calicut University students, recognized by PSC and UPSC, and valid for jobs, competitive exams, and higher studies, including B.Ed. and PG courses like MBA. Exam centers are available in all Kerala districts except Pathanamthitta and Idukki.

What You'll Learn in This Course?

Students will gain comprehensive knowledge in their chosen BA or B.Com subjects, preparing them for professional careers and further studies. The academy provides simplified study materials and expert guidance to ensure success in the entrance exam and degree program.

How to Benefit from This Course

Completing the BA/B.Com program equips students with a recognized undergraduate degree, enabling them to pursue careers in various fields, apply for PSC/UPSC exams, or continue higher studies like B.Ed. or MBA through Calicut University’s affiliated colleges or distance education programs.
